About CorridexAI

CorridexAI Inc. is revolutionizing Canada’s freight logistics by transforming fragmented corridors into a seamlessly coordinated network. Our AI-driven “control tower” platform unifies trucks, trains, and ships, cutting delays by 20–30% and empty miles by up to 66%. Starting in Toronto, Montréal, and Vancouver, we integrate siloed data for real-time visibility, predictive routing, and automated handoffs, driving efficiency and sustainability across North America’s $1.64T trade routes. Led by Alexander Sokolowsky (CEO), Ina Kavelashvili (Montréal-based Director), and Nikoloz Sokolowsky (Data Strategy), CorridexAI is poised to create 25–40 Canadian jobs, secure IRAP grants, and scale globally through TBDC incubation.

Vision & Mission

Vision: CorridexAI transforms Canada’s fragmented freight corridors into a seamlessly coordinated network, using an AI-driven control tower to cut delays by 20–30% and empty miles by up to 66%.

Mission: Starting in Toronto, Montréal, and Vancouver, we unify supply chain stakeholders through a single digital platform, driving efficiency and sustainability across North American trade routes.
